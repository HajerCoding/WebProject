Data Privacy & Credibility

All data provided is aggregated and anonymized to protect farmer privacy. Researchers are the exclusive owners of their private data, and access is granted only for approved academic or environmental studies. This ensures credibility and ethical use in advancing sustainable agriculture in Oman.
